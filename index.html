What is AssistBot?

AssistBot is an AI-powered chatbot designed to help businesses enhance customer service. It can answer FAQs, handle support queries, provide automated responses, and follow up with customers.

Why Choose AssistBot?

  • Instant responses: Answers customer inquiries immediately.
  • 24/7 availability: Works round the clock without breaks.
  • Customizable responses: Allows businesses to modify answers to fit their needs.
  • Automated follow-ups: Sends follow-up messages to ensure customer issues are resolved.
  • Easy integration: Can be embedded into websites and platforms.
  • FAQ database: Learns and improves over time by storing common queries.
  • 5-minute automated check-in: Asks users if their issue was resolved after five minutes.
  • Works in the terminal: Provides API access for developers.
  • Error logging: Tracks errors to ensure smooth functionality.
  • OpenAI-powered: Uses advanced AI for intelligent responses.

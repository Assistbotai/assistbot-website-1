What is AssistBot?

AssistBot is an AI-powered chatbot designed to help businesses enhance customer service. It can answer FAQs, handle support queries, provide automated responses, and follow up with customers.

Why Choose AssistBot?

  1. Instant responses to customer inquiries.
  2. 24/7 availability for continuous support.
  3. Customizable responses to fit your business needs.
  4. Automated follow-ups with customers (including reminders).
  5. Easy integration with websites and platforms.
  6. FAQ database that learns and improves over time.
  7. 5-minute automated check-in to ask if the issue was resolved.
  8. Works in the terminal for developers needing API access.

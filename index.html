What is AssistBot?

AssistBot is an AI-powered chatbot designed to help businesses enhance customer service. It can answer FAQs, handle support queries, and provide automated responses to improve efficiency and customer satisfaction.

Why Choose AssistBot?

  1. Instant responses to customer inquiries.
  2. 24/7 availability for continuous support.
  3. Customizable responses to fit your business needs.
  4. Automated follow-ups for better customer engagement.
  5. Easy integration with websites and platforms.
